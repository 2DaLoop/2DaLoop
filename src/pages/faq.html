Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply sign up, enter your ZIP code and asset type, and start routing electronics today—securely, sustainably, and with no wasted time.

What is the Suggestion Engine?

The Suggestion Engine is an API-powered platform that helps organizations classify, value, and route electronic assets in real time. It instantly connects you with verified recyclers, repair shops, and reintegration partners near you.

Who uses the Suggestion Engine?

  • Government agencies needing secure, compliant decommissioning
  • Universities handling bulk device refreshes
  • Telecoms and enterprises managing reverse logistics
  • Small businesses looking for trusted local recycling and data sanitization partners

What problems does it solve?

  • Eliminates manual vendor searches through outdated databases and SEO-saturated directories
  • Provides one-click access to certified downstream partners
  • Ensures compliance, security, and ESG alignment during decommissioning
  • Reduces delays, overstock, and improper disposal of electronics

How does it work?

  1. Enter your location and asset type (e.g., laptops, servers, batteries).
  2. Instantly see a tailored list of verified facilities filtered by capability, specialty, and proximity.
  3. Route devices to downstream partners with confidence, including data sanitization and hazardous material handling options.

What types of equipment can it handle?

  • Laptops, desktops, and servers
  • Mobile devices and printers
  • Networking and telecom equipment
  • Monitors and storage arrays
  • Batteries, CRTs, and other hazardous materials

How is compliance ensured?

Partners in the network are filtered by industry-recognized certifications (e.g., R2, e-Stewards), hazardous-handling credentials, and cybersecurity frameworks such as CMMC and NIST 800-171. These standards ensure not only that sensitive data and materials are processed securely, but also that cybersecurity protections extend across the supply chain. By aligning with ESG principles, regulatory mandates, and federal cybersecurity requirements, our approach embeds sustainable business practices that safeguard information, reduce risk, and promote long-term resilience.

What are the key benefits?

  • Speed: One-click discovery replaces hours of manual research
  • Security: Certified partners handle data sanitization and hazardous materials
  • Sustainability: Devices are reintegrated into the circular economy, not landfilled
  • Transparency: Built-in ESG reporting supports audit and compliance needs

How much does it cost?

The Suggestion Engine is free to use for upstream providers. There’s no charge to search, filter, and connect with verified downstream partners.
